This data is used exclusively for presenting the functions contained in the app as for example "Invite friends to a voting" or "Show contacts".

Judge it! gets access to the contacts of your address book so that friends using the app will be directly shown to you and so your friends will be notified that you are using the app as well in order to be able to invite you to votings. Judge it! uses the vibration function and acoustic signals to inform you about new votings and messages from your friends in the app. In addition, Judge it! gets access to your camera and photo gallery, so you can also use photos for voting purposes and send photos within the chat function.

Judge it! accesses the memory of the device used, in order to store voting data in an own, private folder and to access that data. Judge it! does not get any kind of access to other data or folders of the device used.

Address data synchronization.

E-mail addresses and telephone numbers from the address book of your device are encrypted and transferred to our servers. Our servers keep the corresponding values in their RAM (random-access-memory) ​​only briefly to identify the list of matching IDs; the data will immediately be discarded after this matching process. At no point in time will the values ​​or the results of the matching progress be written on a hard drive or otherwise be stored.

Google Analytics App Tracking


This app uses Google Analytics App Tracking, an analytics service of Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics App Tracking uses so called „IDs“ that are generated and stored on your device and make it possible to analyze how you use the app. The information generated by the ID on your use of the app are usually transferred to a server of Google in the US and stored there. In case of activation of IP anonymisation within this app, your IP address will be truncated within Member States of the European Union or other parties to the Agreement on the European Economic Area by Google. Only in exceptional cases is the full IP address is transmitted to a Google server in the US and shortened there. Google will use this information on our behalf, to evaluate your use of the app, to compile reports on the activities within the app and to provide other app usage- and internet-related services to us. The IP address that is transferred from your device as part of Google Analytics App Tracking will not be merged with other data of Google.

You can object the capturing of the usage related data generated by the ID (including your IP address) to Google and the processing of these data by Google by activating a switch in the Privacy Policy. Please note that only the measurement of our app is disabled then. It may be that you are still being measured by other providers using the Google Analytics App Tracking.

In this app, the IP-anonymization is activated.
